Madison's Summer at Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code Summer 2016

This has been an amazing summer at Girls Who Code: AT&T Los Angeles.

Some projects I have completed include:

  • Jukebox
  • Madlibs
  • Choose Your Own Adventure
  • Obamacon
  • Bouncing Ball
  • City Scroller

Jukebox: In our final Scratch Project of Week 1, we were assigned to create a program, combined with a visual, will play music with user control. I created my project to look like an iPod which plays three songs, Hotel California-The Eagles, Mirrors-Justin Timberlake, Let it Go-Elsa. This project was created with Scratch.

City Scroller: Similar to most Mario Brothers Games, we have created a background which moves at a different speed. We can also add new buildings which will move at different speeds. City Scroller was created in Python.
